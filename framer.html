ABOUT

For over 15 years, I've lead user-centered design and teams to solve deep problems in complex enterprise spacesthrough empathy, clarity, and strategic leadership, including nearly a decade leading designers at Google and as one of the first two San Francisco designers at Atlassian from 2011-2013.

My last two years at Google I helped lead the first AI efforts for Google’s account managers driving hundreds of millions of demonstrable impact. Originally from Michigan, now based in San Francisco, I am currently taking classes and exploring creative outlets like improv, standup, music production, and screenwriting.

I'm a life-long learner, and love to share my learnings. Drawing on my experience in both systems thinking and improvisational comedy, I approach every project with curiosity. Whether I am architecting a global product or developing a custom React app for my Judo community, my goal is to build experiences that feel honest, functional, and deeply intuitive.

I love to build stuff that works, that matters, and that makes teams and users happy
